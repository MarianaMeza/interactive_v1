Behind the deadliest year in Mexico

In 2017, Mexico recorded its highest homicide rate in years, with a total of 31,902 murders, more than in 2011 at the peak of Mexico’s drug cartel-stoked violence. The death toll is Mexico’s highest since the government began keeping records in 1997, and shot past 2011’s tally of 27,213 homicides.
